Top 3

Halal Cafes in the East



1. BADOQUE CAFÉ



The main reason to visit Badoque café is to badok (eat). From its tantalising spread of savoury dishes, go for their

signatures of the mouth-tingling Kepak Bing Bing (spicy chicken wings) and lamb shank to really enjoy the brand’s

characteristic rich flavours.




The bestseller is the Badoque Ribs ($36), which gave us a bit of a shock, in a good way—it was 5cm thick and almost

as long as my arm. The accompanying brown sauce had a nice silky consistency helped by the addition of beef drippings

and jus. Meat is grilled well-done and served hot to your table with potatoes and greens. We suggest you share your

food with your loved ones — the portions are very generous.



BADOQUE CAFÉ

Address: 298 Bedok Road Singapore 469454

Nearest Station: Tanah Merah

Opening hours: Tue to Sun: 12pm – 11pm

Telephone: 6446 6928



2. Penny University



A mainstay with the Muslim café hopping crowd, Penny University has seen a burgeoning crowd over the years. Brimming

with students and adults alike, patrons hanker for hearty servings of smoky roasts and unique mains, with many downing a

helping of the café’s signature Muddy Espresso to complete their meal.



It is known for its Eggs Benedict, Marmite Cheese toast, coffee, espresso, turkish eggs, muddy espresso with cold milk.

There was a queue of about 5-6 people in front of us when we arrived on a Sunday morning.




Penny University

Address: 402 East Coast Road Singapore 428997

Nearest Station: Kembangan

Opening hours: Daily: 8.30am – 10pm

Telephone: 6345 9055



3. THREE’S A CROWD



At this Halal ice cream café, feel free to enter in groups of three; besides, good things are meant to be shared!

Tucked away in the Tampines heartland, Three’s A Crowd provides an array of gratifyingly creamy flavours that are

sufficiently substantial in taste without leaving a cloyingly saturated aftertaste.



Serving Ice Cream (Single Classic $3, Double Classic $6, Single Premium $4.50, Double Premium $8), Waffles (Maple $6,

Ondeh $9), Brownie ($4.80), Acai (Original $12, Cookie Butter $13), Coffee (Black $4, White $5), Tea (Oolong $5, Chai $6)

and Shakes (Coconut $8, Avocado $8).





Three's A Crowd

Address: Tampines Avenue 4 Blk 802, #01-11 Singapore 520802

Nearest Station: Tampines West

Opening hours: Daily: 12.30pm – 9.30pm

Telephone: 6702 0700
